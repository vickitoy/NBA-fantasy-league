The Bet

Each bettor chooses 10 teams in an S-curve draft. The order is determined by an intense game of paper basketball. The winner's teams has the most cumulative wins at the end of the season.

The Rules

The trade deadline matches the NBA trade deadline which typically follows the All-Star break. The stipulations are as follows:

  • Unlimited 1-for-1 trades that are mutually beneficial for both parties before the trade deadline
  • Maximum of 2 total tilted trades (beneficial for one party), limited to 1 before the trade deadline and 1 after the trade deadline
  • Maximum of 1 trade after the trade deadline
